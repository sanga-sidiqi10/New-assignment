Mantu

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Onion
  • Salt and spices
  • Dumpling wrappers
  • Garlic yogurt sauce

Instructions

  1. Mix meat with onion and spices.
  2. Fill wrappers and fold into dumplings.
  3. Steam for 30 minutes.
  4. Top with garlic yogurt and serve warm.

Firini

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 tbsp corn flour (instead of rice flour)
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • Strawberries (sliced)
  • Bananas (sliced)

Instructions

  1. Mix corn flour with a little cold milk to form a smooth paste.
  2. Heat the rest of the milk with sugar and cardamom.
  3. Add corn flour mixture slowly, stirring constantly.
  4. Cook until thickened.
  5. Pour into bowls and cool.
  6. Top with sliced strawberries.
  7. Then add sliced bananas one by one for decoration.

Author: Sanga Sidiqi!
